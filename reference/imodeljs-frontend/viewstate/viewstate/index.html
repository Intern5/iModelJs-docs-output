    🔗ViewState Class

    The state of a ViewDefinition element, loaded in the frontend. ViewDefinitions specify the area/volume that is viewed, and points to a DisplayStyle and a CategorySelector. Subclasses of ViewDefinition determine which model(s) are viewed.
